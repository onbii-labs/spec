# 0005: Not An AI Meeting-Notes Product

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-21

## Context

Onbii began from frustration with products that record meetings, transcribe them, summarise them, and keep the resulting notes inside their own application or cloud service.

Those products can be useful, but their goal often ends at a note or summary. The Onbii conversation moved toward something broader: portable personal knowledge that can accumulate context, links, provenance, and reuse over time.

## Decision

Onbii should not be framed as an AI meeting-notes product.

Onbii is an open specification and local-first personal knowledge infrastructure for representing, preserving, linking, inspecting, and reprocessing knowledge objects.

## Consequences

- Meeting notes are an important use case, not the product category.
- AI features should support knowledge preservation and recall rather than define the project.
- Documentation should avoid implying that summaries are the final output.
- Object types beyond meetings can be added later without changing the project identity.
