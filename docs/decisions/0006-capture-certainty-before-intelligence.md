# 0006: Capture Certainty Before Intelligence

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-21

## Context

The first clear user value is confidence that important conversations, calls, memos, and recordings are not being lost.

Advanced summaries, graph views, AI recall, rich classification, and deep linking can create later value, but they do not matter if the original material never enters a user-owned archive.

## Decision

Onbii should prioritise capture certainty before advanced intelligence.

The early experience should make a person feel that a recording or conversation is safely in Onbii, inspectable, and available to revisit later.

## Consequences

- Early milestones should focus on preserving real source material and producing a minimally useful transcript or view.
- Rich summaries, templates, provider selection, graphing, and advanced classification can come later.
- Roadmap language should describe when Onbii becomes useful, not just when technical layers are complete.
- Manual workflows may be useful for examples, but the desired direction remains lower-friction capture and ingestion.
