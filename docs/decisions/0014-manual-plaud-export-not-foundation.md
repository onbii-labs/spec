# 0014: Manual Plaud Export Is Not The Foundation

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-21

## Context

Plaud was one of the starting points for the conversation because its hardware and microphones are useful, but its notes and workflows can feel trapped in Plaud's system.

Manual export may be possible, but relying on users to repeatedly export and move files does not match the desired low-friction experience.

## Decision

Manual Plaud export should not be treated as the foundation of Onbii's desired workflow.

It may still be useful for examples, experiments, fallback material, or early testing before better source paths exist.

## Consequences

- Roadmap and examples should avoid implying that manual Plaud export is the intended long-term user experience.
- Plaud can remain a source candidate, but Onbii should not depend on Plaud openness that does not exist.
- First-stage examples may use manually supplied recordings when the learning goal is the object format rather than capture automation.
